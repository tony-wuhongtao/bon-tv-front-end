ABOUT BON TV

Funded by venture capital firm CDH Venture Partners and independent in its editorial control, Blue Ocean Network (BON) is the first and only private English-language Television Network to offer International viewers comprehensive and objective programming produced exclusively from and about China.

Until now international audiences have had limited access to independent, quality programming from this fascinating country and BON’s exclusive content provides an entertaining and engaging way for international viewers to expand their views and understanding of China and its people.

For everything from modern art to business etiquette, from cuisine to technology, BON is your best guide for all things business, culture and lifestyle from China.

Produced by an international team of media professionals at China’s top private media company, BON covers the country in a way never attempted before with mold-breaking, innovative programming presented in an objective, balanced and professional style.

BON is available on Dish Network USA as part of the International Basic Package and Chinese Package, on Sky TV (BSkyB) across Great Britain as part of the Basic Package, on MHz Networks USA in Washington DC, and on Broadcast Stations and Cable Networks across more than 30 U.S. States, and on WNYJ New York and all the major cable channels in the NY, NJ and CT Tri-State area.

Additionally, BON is accessible via Galaxy 23 across all of North America, Mexico and the Caribbean; Astra 2F across Europe; and on Apstar-7 across Asia-Pacific, Australasia and the Middle East. Please see our latest coverage detailed below.
